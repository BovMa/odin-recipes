Lasagna

Quick & Easy Lasagne

Servings: 4–6
Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Total Time: 45 mins

Ingredients:

  • 9 no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or turkey
  • 1 jar (24 oz / 680g) marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg (optional, for ricotta mixture)
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Olive oil (for cooking)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the meat: In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add ground meat, season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add sauce: Pour in the marinara sauce, stir, and let simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Make ricotta mix (optional): In a bowl, mix ricotta with the egg, a pinch of salt, and a little Parmesan.
  5. Assemble the lasagne:
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
    • Add 3 lasagna noodles.
    • Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture, then meat sauce, then sprinkle mozzarella.
    • Repeat layers two more times (noodles → ricotta → sauce → mozzarella).
    • Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  6. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  7. Rest and serve: Let sit for 5–10 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
